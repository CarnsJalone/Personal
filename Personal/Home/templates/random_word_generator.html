Random Word/Name Generator

Here are a few fun applications that I created. The first two randomly generate a sequence of consonants and vowels. While what they produce will unlikely be in the Merriam-Webster unabridged, it demonstrates the power, and potential application of random sequence generators. The third application is a simple random name generator. It looks at two texts files, first names and last names, and they randomly selects one entry from both, providing a completely random full name.
